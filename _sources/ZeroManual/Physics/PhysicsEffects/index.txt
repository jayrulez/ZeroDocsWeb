Physics Effects
---------------

.. rst-class:: SearchDescription

Physics effects apply forces based effects such as flow or buoyancy to specific object, regions, or spaces. Before talking about physics effects it is necessary to understand the **Region** component. Then we can learn about the various physics effects that exist in Zero.
