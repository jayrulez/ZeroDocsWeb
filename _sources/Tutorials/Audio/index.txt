Audio
=====

.. rst-class:: SearchDescription

Sound effects and music are an important part of any game. The Zero Engine supports both 2D & 3D positional sound among a variety of effects. While most games can get by with just using **SoundEmitter**, **SimpleSound**, and **SoundListener** larger projects may want to take advantage of **SoundTags**.
